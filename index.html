Lead Organizers Partners

Bamikole Omishore

Special Advisor to the Governor on SDGs and Multilateral Relations.

Bamikole Omishore, is a seasoned International Relations and Digital Communication Specialist, with expertise in managing government public relationship, policy formulation, advocacy, political campaigns and media relations.

Azeez Badmus

Special Adviser to the governor on Innovation, Science, Technology and Digital Economy

Azeez Badmus has innovative mind and full passion for Information Technology, he has become a top notch professional and a significant figure in the field of technology and digital innovation. His commitment, using technology for Social Development, its role in transformation of government processes and his involvement in the flagship initiatives provide significant changes.

Adewumi Adeyemi

Member of Osun State Assembly

Adewumi Adeyemi, also known as Irekandu, is a dedicated scholar and community leader. His commitment to fostering a better Nigeria is evident through his extensive involvement in education and community development initiatives. His relentless dedication to his community and his visionary leadership continue to inspire many, making significant strides in both legislative and grassroots development.
