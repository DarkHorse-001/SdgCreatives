Olalekan Badmus (Phoenix)

Digital Media Strategist

Olalekan Badmus (Phoenix) is a digital media expert and Data Analyst. With a broad background in corporate finance and investment, Olalekan has sufficient experience in banking, investment and public relations. He has managed several brands in public and private sector and currently leads the digital media unit of the current Executive Governor of Osun State. Olalekan is passionate about Sustainable Development Goals and promoting the creative industry and has volunteered for projects towards realizing the SDG goals.

Mogbolahan Ajala

Lead Consultant

Mogbolahan Ajala is a formally trained public sector Architect, Urban Planner, and cultural heritage professional. He possesses over Sixteen years of experience in public infrastructure-based policy and contract administration at both the subnational and national government levels. He is currently an official consultant to the Osun State Government, specifically within the Ministry of Tourism, Arts and Culture. His primary responsibility involves the development of a cultural Heritage Trail for economic expansion and job creation opportunities. Academically, Mogbolahan Ajala holds a Bachelor of Science in Architecture from Covenant University, Ota, Ogun State, Nigeria. Furthermore, he possesses a Master's degree in Urban and Regional Planning (MURP) from the University of Lagos, Lagos, Nigeria.
